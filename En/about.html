About Us

Agromuz Ice Cream Company

Established in 2010 and based in the heart of Uzbekistan, Agromuz has been a proud producer of exceptional ice creams from our factory in Samarkand, Samarkand viloyati, Yangijoy Street. Under the leadership of our factory director, Umar Mukhammadiev, we have become a household name, known for crafting a wide variety of delicious and popular ice creams. At Agromuz, we blend tradition with innovation, offering an array of flavors to delight every palate. From timeless classics to unique creations, our commitment to quality and taste has made us a favorite among ice cream lovers across theregion. Agromuz – proudly bringing the taste of joy from Samarkand to your table!

Our Features

At Agromuz, we pride ourselves on offering a unique ice cream experience through a combination of quality, innovation, and variety. Here are the key features that set us apart:

Wide Range of Flavors: From timeless classics to bold new creations, we offer something for every taste.
High-Quality Ingredients: Every scoop is crafted with only the finest ingredients to ensure exceptional flavor and quality.
Commitment to Tradition and Innovation: We blend the best of traditional recipes with fresh, modern ideas to create unforgettable ice cream experiences.
